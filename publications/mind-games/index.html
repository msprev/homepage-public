“Mind games”

Mark Sprevak

Final version to be in
Turing and Wittgenstein on Mind and Mathematics
(edited by D. Proudfoot)
Oxford University Press: Oxford

The chapter will (1) introduce Turing’s and Wittgenstein’s rival approaches to understanding the human mind and psychology; (2) briefly sketch the very different legacies of the two approaches today; (3) explore some of the interesting interactions between Turing and Wittgenstein and the influence they had on each other while in Cambridge concerning computational approaches to the mind.
