I am Senior Lecturer in Philosophy at the University of Edinburgh.

My primary research interests are in philosophy of mind, philosophy of science, metaphysics, and philosophy of language. I am interested in how these issues in these areas intersect with cognitive science.

I did my Ph.D. in 2005 with Professor Peter Lipton at the

Current research

  2. Foundations of computation and computational theories of mind

  3. Mental representation

  4. Bayesian inference
