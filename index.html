I am Senior Lecturer in Philosophy at the School of Philosophy, Psychology and Language Sciences (PPLS), University of Edinburgh.

My primary research interests are in philosophy of mind, philosophy of science, metaphysics, and philosophy of language. I am interested in how these issues in these areas intersect with cognitive science.

Current research areas:

  1. Embodied, extended, and distributed cognition

  2. Using computation to explain cognition

  3. The role of sub-personal representations in cognitive science

Current projects:
