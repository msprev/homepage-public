I was proud to win the EUSA Teaching award for ‘Best Course’ in University of Edinburgh for my course ‘Ethics of Artificial Intelligence’.

I have previously been nominated for ‘Best Course’ (19 times); ‘Best Teacher’ (8 times); ‘Best Supervisor’ and ‘Best Practice in Inclusive Learning and Teaching’.

I have taught students on degree programmes in Philosophy, Psychology, Informatics, Cognitive Science, Law, and Business Studies.
